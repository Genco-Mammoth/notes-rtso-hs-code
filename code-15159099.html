CN 1515 90 99 — Refined Oils, Solid >1 kg or Fluid

MFN: 9.6% RU/BY: 50% Indonesia: 9.6% No GSP for Indonesia

This code covers refined or otherwise non-crude fixed vegetable or microbial fats and oils for food, cosmetic, or pharmaceutical use (non-technical), in bulk packaging (solid in packings > 1 kg, or in liquid/fluid form). It carries a higher MFN rate than its crude counterpart (1515 90 59), reflecting the EU's higher protection for processed/finished goods.

Full Legal Description

CN 1515 90 99 00 / 9920 / 9992 / 9999

“Other fixed vegetable fats and oils and their fractions, whether or not refined, but not chemically modified — Other oils and their fractions — Other — Solid, other; fluid”

This covers refined or otherwise non-crude fixed vegetable/microbial fats and oils (excluding crude oils, which are 1515 90 59) that are not for technical or industrial use, and are in solid packings > 1 kg or in fluid/liquid form.

Bottom line for Indonesia
Indonesia pays the full MFN rate of 9.6%. The same GSP Section 3 graduation applies. This rate is 3.2 percentage points higher than the crude equivalent (6.4% for 1515 90 59), reflecting the EU's higher tariff wall for processed/finished goods.

Policy logic
The 3.2 percentage-point gap between crude and refined rates follows a standard EU tariff escalation pattern: raw materials face lower duties (encouraging imports for EU processing), while finished/processed goods face higher duties (protecting EU processors who add value).
