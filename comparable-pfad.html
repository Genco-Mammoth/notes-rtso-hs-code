PFAD — Palm Fatty Acid Distillate

CN 3823 19 30 MFN: 2.9% No ADD (outside scope) GSP: 0% (Section 6 eligible)

By-product of physical refining of palm oil — the volatile fatty acids stripped during steam deodorisation. Widely used as biodiesel feedstock. At 2.9% MFN (0% with GSP), it is one of the cheapest legal paths for Indonesian-origin fatty products. See the full code analysis for TARIC subcodes, ADD details, and RSO relevance.

Bottom line
PFAD is cheaper than RSO (0% GSP now, 2.9% MFN from Jan 2027) and has massive supply. But it requires a different processing route (esterification) that not all biodiesel plants can handle. RSO processes like a standard triglyceride oil — simpler for most plants.
