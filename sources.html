Source Discrepancy Analysis

Discrepancy 1: ERGA OMNES rate for 1515 90 59 / 99

⚠ Major discrepancy across databases
Source Rate shown for 1515 90 59 Rate shown for 1515 90 99 Legal basis cited
EUR-Lex (Reg. 2024/1652) 6.4% 9.6% Reg. 2024/1652, Annex, fn. (6)
tariffnumber.com (2025/2026) 6.4% 9.6% Reg. 2024/1652
celna24.pl 0% (excl. RU/BY) 0% (excl. RU/BY) Reg. 2024/1652 (interpreted differently)
Resolution: The EUR-Lex text is authoritative. Regulation 2024/1652 explicitly states 6,4 [(6)] and 9,6 [(6)] for these codes. The celna24.pl interpretation (that the rates are 0% for non-RU/BY origins) is not supported by the regulation text. The rates stated in the regulation Annex are the legally applicable conventional duty rates for all origins, with the exception that RU/BY goods face 50% instead.

This report uses 6.4% and 9.6% as the MFN rates.

Discrepancy 2: GSP rate for sensitive products

⚠ Minor discrepancy in GSP sensitive rate
Source GSP rate for 1515 90 59 GSP rate for 1515 90 99 Basis
celna24.pl 2.9% 6.1% MFN − 3.5 pp
This report (calculation) 2.9% 6.1% Same: 6.4% − 3.5 = 2.9%; 9.6% − 3.5 = 6.1%
Resolution: No actual discrepancy — both agree. The GSP sensitive rate is calculated by subtracting 3.5 percentage points from the MFN rate (Article 7 of Reg. 978/2012). However, this rate is not available to Indonesia due to Section 3 graduation.

Discrepancy 3: Pre-July 2024 rates

⚠ Unresolved: what were the rates before Reg. 2024/1652?

Whether CN 1515 90 59 and 1515 90 99 were duty-free (0%) or already carried the 6.4%/9.6% rates before 1 July 2024 is not definitively resolved by the primary sources available in this research.

The regulation recitals suggest that many of the affected products had rates “set at zero or very low,” but do not explicitly state the prior rate for each individual CN code. Resolving this would require consulting the CCT Annex I as it stood on 30 June 2024 (the consolidated version of Reg. 2658/87 before the Reg. 2024/1652 amendment took effect).

Impact on this report: Minimal. The rates from 1 July 2024 onward (6.4% and 9.6%) are firmly established. The pre-July 2024 rate is a historical curiosity, not a current operational concern.

Methodology

How this report was compiled
  1. Primary source identification: Identified the foundational regulations (2658/87, 2024/1652, 978/2012) via EUR-Lex as the authoritative basis.
  2. Rate extraction: Extracted duty rates directly from the regulation text (Reg. 2024/1652 Annex, Chapter 15 entries).
  3. Cross-reference: Verified rates against tariffnumber.com and celna24.pl for consistency.
  4. Discrepancy resolution: Where sources conflicted, analyzed the regulation text and recitals to determine the correct interpretation.
  5. GSP verification: Confirmed Indonesia's Section 3 graduation status via the GSP Hub and the legal provisions of Reg. 978/2012.
  6. Historical comparison: Compared rates at three reference points (Jan 2025, Jan 2026, Jul 2026) using annual CN regulations.
  7. Verification date: All data verified as of 4 July 2026.
